HOW DOES IT WORK?

1. Sign up with LinkedIn

Sign up to Ozaur using your LinkedIn account. Your profile is now automaticcaly created, using your LinkedIn data. We do not display any of your contact information. If you want, you can additionally write short paragraph about yourself.

We will send you email as a confirmation. Please, respond to it with simpy one world. It will make use activated user.

Voila. Now you can start browsing another people profiles. Somebody's expertise caught you attention? You would love to send this person email and get response? It's time to buy bitcoins!

2. Buy bitcoins

In order to start bidding another people attention, you need to have bitcoins. You can easily buy them via your Ozaur account. We have integrated Coinbase wallet for you. It allows to safely buy, use and accept bitcoin currency.

3. Bidding attention

On everyone's profiles you will see, how much the others wants to pay for certain user attention. Now it's time to decide, how much it is worth for you. Simply click and share amount of bitcoin, which you want to pay for messaging this person. This amount will be now "freezed" until to end of bidding. It means, that you will not be able to use it. You can always come back and wager more.

Bidding is solved every week. If amount which you are willing to pay is the highest, you win. Now you can send message to this person. We will pass it via our system, so you will never see each other emails. No spam for the future:)

4. Paying for the attention

Since you win auction, you have 48 hours to formulate an email. Try to write shortly and precisely. Person, to whom it will be send also have 48 hours for response.

If everything is fine with a message, bitcoins are charged from you account. But if there is any problem with response (abusement, no response, empty response) we will not charge you. Person will not receive your money. Amount which you wagered will be "unfreezed".
